About Us

Our Mission

Becoming a world leader in energy research, education, technology, and energy systems analysis.

Read more

Our Vision

  • Coordinate and initiate collaborative interdisciplinary energy research among the universities and the energy industry.
  • Share research results with a wide audience, including the science community, media, business, governments, and industry.
  • Assist in the creation and development of a Florida-based energy technology industry.
  • Provide a state resource for objective energy systems analysis.
  • Develop education and outreach programs to prepare a qualified energy workforce and informed public.
